Create a 3×3 pixel-art sprite sheet for a retro dark-fantasy dungeon crawler enemy called Heavy.

Layout:

- 3 rows = enemy variants. Entities on the same row are the same entity across the 3 columns and should look the same, just in different poses. Each separate row is a different entity and looks different. 
- 3 columns = poses
    - Column 1: Idle (front-facing)
    - Column 2: Attack (front-facing)
    - Column 3: Dead

Critical orientation rules:

- Idle and attack sprites must face perfectly straight forward.
- The enemy’s shoulders must be symmetrical.
- The head must point directly at the viewer.
- The sprite must look like a first-person game target standing directly in front of the player.

Attack animation:
- wields a heavy mace. 
- The weapon must not be pointed toward the viewer.

Dead pose:
- May fall sideways, forward, or backward.
- Orientation unrestricted.
- appears as a pile of parts instead of a body

Enemy design:
- A mechanical juggernaut, steel, iron, and tungsten colors
- Muted palette fitting a dungeon crawler with a serious tone
- Exposed metal parts and mechanisms.

Style:
- Retro pixel-art game sprite
- Dark adult dungeon crawler tone
- Clean readable silhouette
- Rendering constraints:
- Transparent background
- Each sprite clearly separated
- Consistent scale
- PNG sprite-sheet style

Negative constraints:
- No side-facing sprites
- No angled attack poses
- No glowing eyes
- No bright magical effects
- No background
- No scenery, floor, border, or backdrop
- No motion blur
- No turning of the body.
- No side view. No 3/4 view. No turned torso. No angled stance.
